Pick a name

What is a character without a name? The right name can be that little something that sums up who the character is or wants to be. There are thousands of names from dozens of cultures—historical and fictional—from which you can choose. Making up your own is also, of course, an option.

Extras: Character Concept

Remember that one of the conceits of the game is that your character begins as an unknown and struggling adventurer. You don’t start play as the crown prince or an archmage. You must earn your honors through deeds, and you can be sure there’ll be a price. So, start thinking about who your character is and how they became an adventurer.
