Player Summary

Character Sheet

Your character sheet is the cornerstone of your Player Character, or PC. You’ll need it for each and every game session, along with three six-sided dice (Fantasy AGE doesn’t use any other kind), one of which should look different from the others. You’ll also need something to take notes with. A pencil and some scratch paper is ideal.

Tips: Gameplay

Some groups use miniature figures and maps to note where characters are, but you can play Fantasy AGE perfectly well without them by imagining where characters and objects are, what some call “theater of the mind.”
